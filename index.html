Meet Your Provider

Rooted in Faith and Expertise

Dr. RNyakako
DNP · PMHNP-BC · AGPCNP-BC

With over a decade of clinical nursing experience and dual board certification in psychiatric and adult gerontology care, Dr. RNyakako brings a uniquely comprehensive perspective to mental health treatment.

Her practice is rooted in a deep conviction that true healing addresses the whole person — mind, body, and spirit. Drawing on her faith, clinical rigor, and compassionate approach, she creates a sanctuary where patients feel seen, heard, and genuinely cared for.

Her doctoral research explores mental health stigma among the Jamaican diaspora, reflecting her commitment to culturally responsive, inclusive care.
